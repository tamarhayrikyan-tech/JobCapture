# Privacy Policy for JobCapture

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

JobCapture ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

JobCapture collects and stores **only** the following data **locally on your device**:

1. **Job posting information you choose to save:**
   - Job title/position
   - Company name
   - Job posting URL
   - Location
   - Company address
   - Salary information
   - Application requirements
   - Deadline dates
   - Date posted
   - Date applied
   - Follow up
   - Result
   - Personal notes you add

2. **No other data is collected.** We do not collect:
   - Your browsing history
   - Your personal information (name, email, etc.)
   - Your search queries
   - Analytics or usage statistics
   - Cookies or tracking data

### How Data is Stored

- **All data is stored locally** using Chrome's built-in storage API
- Data **never leaves your computer**
- No data is sent to our servers (we don't have servers)
- No data is sent to third parties
- No cloud backup or syncing (unless you export CSV yourself)

### How Data is Used

Your captured job data is used **only** to:
- Display your saved jobs in the tracker interface
- Allow you to edit and manage your job applications
- Export to CSV when you request it

That's it. Your data is yours, period.

## Data You Provide

When you click "Capture" on a job posting:
- The extension reads **only the current tab** you're viewing
- It extracts job details from that specific page
- It stores that information locally
- **Nothing is sent anywhere**

## Third-Party Access

**JobCapture does NOT:**
- Share data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your behavior
- Send data to analytics services

**We literally can't access your data** because it never leaves your device.

## Chrome Permissions Explained

JobCapture requests the following Chrome permissions:

### activeTab
**Why:** To read job posting information from the page you're currently viewing when you click "Capture"

**What it does:** Allows the extension to see the content of the active tab

**What it doesn't do:** Does not allow background tracking or access to other tabs

### storage
**Why:** To save captured jobs on your computer

**What it does:** Stores your job data locally using Chrome's storage API

**What it doesn't do:** Does not send data to external servers

### scripting
**Why:** To extract job details from web pages

**What it does:** Runs code on the current page to find job information

**What it doesn't do:** Does not run automatically in background; only runs when you click "Capture"

## Data Security

- Data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
- Data is only accessible to the extension on your device
- No passwords or sensitive personal information is collected
- If you uninstall the extension, all data is deleted

## Legal Basis (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Union:

**Legal basis for processing:** Consent
- You explicitly choose what data to capture
- You can delete data anytime
- You can export data anytime (data portability)

**Your GDPR rights:**
- Right to access: View your data in the tracker
- Right to rectification: Edit any field inline
- Right to erasure: Delete jobs or uninstall extension
- Right to data portability: Export to CSV
- Right to object: Don't use the extension
- Right to withdraw consent: Uninstall the extension

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents:

**We do not:**

**Your CCPA rights:**
- Right to know: This policy explains everything we collect
- Right to delete: Uninstall extension or delete individual jobs
- Right to opt-out of sale: Not applicable (we don't sell data)
